What is a sport motorcycle?

Sport motorcycles are designed for speed. They are designed to take you from Point A to Point B quickly. There are several types of sport motorcycles: lightweights, supersports, and superbike. We at Vicious Cycles focus on lightweight and supersport motorcycles.
